About ABES EC

ABES Engineering College (ABESEC) is a private engineering college in Ghaziabad, Uttar Pradesh, India, established in 2000. Affiliated with Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam Technical University, Lucknow (AKTU), it offers various undergraduate (B.Tech) and postgraduate (M.Tech, MBA, MCA) programs in engineering and management. ABESEC is known for its strong focus on placements and industry-oriented training
