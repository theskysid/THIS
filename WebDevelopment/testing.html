You got some Qs, We have the As

Got questions? Find quick answers here.

Who can participate in the Hackathon?

All undergraduate students in the region, regardless of discipline, experience, or skills, are welcome to participate, requiring only an inquisitive mind and a willingness to learn.

What is the registration Procedure?

Registrations are Live on Devfolio, "Apply With Devfolio" button is present on the front page of website

Is there any Registration fee ?

Participation in Aurora Hackathon is completely free. All we ask for is your time, enthusiasm, and creative efforts.

Are there specific themes?

Aurora is primarily centered on Web3, but remains open to embracing Open Innovation in AI-ML, FinTech, and other related fields.

What is the team size for the hackathon?

Teams need to have 2 to 4 members when submitting a project.

Do I will have rights of my own code?

Absolutely! Submission only grants usage rights for evaluation and award purposes, ensuring you maintain full ownership.

Will there be certificates/goodies provided to the participants?

Yes, participation certificates and goodies will be rewarded to all the participants.

How can we get updates?

Follow our social media platforms for real-time updates!
